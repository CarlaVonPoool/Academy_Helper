# Outgoing invoices

Article Description: Efficient receivables management: Steps to smoothly prepare outgoing invoices in Poool
Last Updated: 4. August 2021
Published: No
Suggested: No

With Poool's **billing assistant**, you automatically link external services, costs, hourly bookings and partial invoices for correct billing and tracking. You can also enter individual services, prices and quantities for **customized invoices**. Be careful to **check** to avoid errors. The **billing assistant** and your **price list** help keep data **consistent** and **accurate**.

![Untitled](Outgoing%20invoices/Untitled.png)

1. Navigate to `Accounting → Outgoing invoices`. 
2. Select a **project** from the drop-down menu and click `+ Invoice` to create a new **invoice**.

![Untitled](Outgoing%20invoices/Untitled%201.png)

1. Fill in the **necessary information** for the invoice, such as customer information, responsibilities and the invoice date.

![Untitled](Outgoing%20invoices/Untitled%202.png)

1. Use the **billing assistant** to **automatically link** external services, costs, hourly bookings and partial invoices.
2. Click **billing assistant** and select the items you **want to include** in the invoice.

![Untitled](Outgoing%20invoices/Untitled%203.png)

1. The **selected items** are **automatically** inserted into the invoice. Check the **information** and adjust it if necessary.
2. If you want to create an **individual invoice**, you can manually enter services, prices and quantities. Be sure to check all **information** carefully to avoid errors.
3. **Save** the invoice by clicking `Save`.
4. Check the **invoice** you have created and make sure all the information is **correct**. If everything is fine, click on `Billing` to give the invoice an **invoice number**.

![Untitled](Outgoing%20invoices/Untitled%204.png)

<aside>
ℹ️ With these steps, you have successfully created an **outgoing invoice** with Poool and used the **billing assistant** to ensure that the items are **correctly linked**. By using the billing assistant and your price list, you can ensure that all your **data is consistent and accurate**, reducing the **administrative burden** and avoiding errors.

</aside>
