# Contract clearing

Article Description: Accurate billing: Steps towards precise invoicing of orders in Poool
Last Updated: 4. August 2021
Published: No
Suggested: No

In Poool, there is **blanket** and **expenditure-based** order accounting. You can schedule **partial** and **final invoices**. With one click, you can create invoices from defined parts, individually or from the order. The **billing assistant** (magic wand) helps with billable hours and third-party services. You can also create **individual invoices**, not just with **services** from the **price list**.

1. Navigate to the order area in the software under `Projects → Orders` and open the desired order.
2. Select the billing method you want to use:
    1. Order includes hours (blanket)
    2. Billing hours directly (expenditure-based)
    3. Do not bill hours
3. To create a **billing** for this project, go to the **billing area** of the project (`tab: Invoices`).
4. Create a new invoice via `+ outgoing invoice` and enter an invoice title.
5. Use the **billing assistant** in the invoice calculation to add billable **hours** and **external services** to the bill.
6. Optionally, you can create an invoice completely **individually** and not only use the services from the price list. Adjust the invoice according to **your needs**.
7. Check the **draft invoice** and make sure all necessary information is correct.
8. After reviewing the **draft invoice**, click on `→ Billing` to assign an **invoice number** to the invoice.
9. **Save** the generated invoice by clicking the `Save` button.

<aside>
ℹ️ Now you can export the invoice as a PDF, print it or send it by e-mail to the customer by selecting the appropriate options in the `Documents` tab of the calculation.

</aside>
