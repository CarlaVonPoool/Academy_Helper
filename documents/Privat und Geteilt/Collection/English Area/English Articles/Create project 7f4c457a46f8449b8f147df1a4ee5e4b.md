## Create project

<aside>
💡 The project serves as a super-folder, where all the calculations, invoices, costs and hours are collected.

</aside>

In Poool, you create a **project** by entering important information such as the **customer** and the **project name**. You then create **tasks** and **tickets** and assign employees. You will be able to maintain an eye over the progress of the project with a **detailed plan**. Poool dashboards and reports help you to **monitor** and **optimise** everything.

1. **Navigate** to the project area under `Projects → Management`.

![Untitled](Create%20project/Untitled.png)

1. Click on the `+ Project` button.

![Untitled](Create%20project/Untitled%201.png)

1. Enter the relevant information such as **customer**, **project name** and **other details**.

![Untitled](Create%20project/Untitled%202.png)

1. Assign **responsibilities** to employees to the project by entering the employees in the corresponding fields for Responsible / Co-responsible.
2. Track the **project progress** and ensure that the project is completed on time and within budget by regularly reviewing the **project plan**.
3. Use Poool dashboards and **reports** to **monitor** and optimise project progress and associated metrics. Navigate to the **Dashboard** section or the **Reporting** section and create a **project dashboard** or a **project report**.
4. **Save** the information entered regularly by clicking on the `Save` button.

<aside>
ℹ️ With these steps, you have successfully created a project in Poool and can track and optimize the progress of the project to ensure that the project is completed on time and budget.

</aside>
