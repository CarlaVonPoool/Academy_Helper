## Create order

<aside>
💡 In Poool, offers are “contracted” and automatically reworded into orders.

Other settings, such as order planning, can then be made in the orders.

</aside>

In Poool, you can order **offers completely** or just **individual item groups**. Optional item groups offer **flexibility** without unnecessarily increasing the total amount. Customers decide whether they want to take them into account. This allows you to **customize** the offer to project requirements and budget.

1. Navigate to the **Offer area** under `Projects → Offers` in the software and open the desired offer.

![Untitled](Order/Untitled.png)

1. Review the offer and pay attention to the **item groups** included, including optional item groups
2. To start the **ordering process**, change the status to `Ordered`.

![Untitled](Order/Untitled%201.png)

1. This opens the **commissioning process**, where you can choose whether you want to commission the **entire offer**, include or omit optional items and whether you want to commission the **offer partially**.

![Untitled](Order/Untitled%202.png)

1. Decide on the **desired commissioning option**: Total commissioning (Ok), partial commissioning (Advanced group commissioning) or the inclusion of optional item groups.
2. Confirm the order and save the **created orders** by clicking on the `→ OK` button.
3. Manage the **created orders** and monitor the progress in the **project or order area** of the Poool software.

<aside>
ℹ️ By following these steps, you have successfully commissioned offers in Poool, enabling you and your customers to optimize the offer to suit the respective project requirements and the available budget.

</aside>
