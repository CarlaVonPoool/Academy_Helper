## Calculate offer

<aside>
💡 Offers are created under a project and filled with item groups and items.

Item groups can be set up optionally with individual tax rates.

</aside>

In Poool, you can **create and calculate** an offer by filling in the required fields such as **customer information, offer title** and **offer date**. Optionally, you can also insert **services** from the **rate card** or define and insert **calculation templates** to save time and effort.

1. Navigate to the **offer** **area** under `Projects → Offers`.

![Untitled](Order/Untitled.png)

1. Select the **project** for which you want to write an **offer**.
2. Click on the `+ Offer` button.

![Untitled](Calculate%20offer/Untitled.png)

1. Fill in the required fields, such as **customer information**, **offer title** and **offer date**.
2. Create a **group** by clicking on the blue `+`. Give the group a title and save it.
3. Add **services / items** from the rate card within the created group by opening the **service drop-down**. Select the desired **services / items** and click on them. The stored service is displayed without a number.
4. Enter the desired **number** and complete your **offer**.
    
    ![Untitled](Calculate%20offer/Untitled%201.png)
    

1. Alternatively, you can use **preconfigured templates**, which already contain a predefined **item group** with all services. Click on the black arrow pointing downwards and select the desired template.

![Untitled](Calculate%20offer/Untitled%202.png)

1. Check the **calculation** and adjust it if necessary.
2. **Save** the offer by clicking on the `Save` button.
3. Optionally, you can export the offer as a **PDF, print** it or **send it** to the customer by e-mail by selecting the appropriate options.

<aside>
ℹ️ With these steps, you have successfully created and calculated a offer in Poool, taking into account the creation of position groups or the use of pre-configured templates. You can now efficiently manage your offers and send them to customers.

</aside>
