# Book project times

Article Description: Improve your time tracking: steps to smoothly book project times in Poool
Last Updated: 4. August 2021
Published: Yes
Suggested: No

In Poool, **project times can be booked based on performance** by selecting a target (e.g. project or ticket), specifying the **required time** and an **individual performance**. The booked times can be viewed in **hour management** and **linked** to a new invoice in the project for **expense-related billing**. The **Invoicing wizard** helps with **adding** the billable hours.

### **Project times can be booked in two ways:**

![Untitled](Book%20project%20times/Untitled.png)

### **Method 1:** Via the **Quick Access Menu**

1. Click on the **clock icon** in the quick access menu located on the right side of the user interface.
2. Select a **destination** for your times, e.g. a project or ticket.
3. Enter the required time either **directly** or via the **incremental increment** using the buttons.
4. Specify the **description of the activity** in the comment field.
5. Optionally select another **service**.
6. Save the entry via `→ Record time`.

![Untitled](Book%20project%20times/Untitled%201.png)

### **Method 2:** Via the **Time recording**

1. Navigate to `Time tracking` in the software.
2. Switch to the `Project time` grid
3. Click on `Project recording +` to create a new line for the **recording of project times**.

![Untitled](Book%20project%20times/Untitled%202.png)

1. Select **a target for** your times, e.g. a project or ticket.
2. Enter the **required time** and click on `Save` to record the time.

![Untitled](Book%20project%20times/Untitled%203.png)

The **booked times** can be viewed in the **time management**. Navigate to `Accounting → Time management` to get an **overview** of all **booked times**.
