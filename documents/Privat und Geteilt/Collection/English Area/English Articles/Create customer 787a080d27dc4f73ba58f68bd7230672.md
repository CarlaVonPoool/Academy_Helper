## Create customer

<aside>
💡 Customers are created as a contact in the address book and then activated as a customer and/or supplier.

</aside>

When **creating a new customer**, fill in important fields such as **company name** and **abbreviation**. Tags will help you to find your customer easier.

Enter **contact options** such as telephone numbers and e-mail addresses and add **address information**. You can activate your new customer under the `Client` tab. Options such as payment terms and VAT rates are available in this tab. 

1. **Navigate** to the address book area under `CRM → Address Book`.
    
    ![Untitled](Create%20customer/Untitled.png)
    

1. **Click** on the `+ Contact` button.
2. Fill in the **mandatory** fields, including the official **company name** and **abbreviation**.
3. Add **optional tags** to make the contact easier to find.
4. Enter **contact options** such as telephone numbers and e-mail addresses.
5. Add **address information** such as street, city and postcode if required.
6. **Activate the contact** as a customer by ticking the corresponding checkbox `→ activate` in the `Client` tab.
7. After the contact has been activated as a **customer**, configure individual **payment targets**, **rate cards** and **tax rates** if required.
8. **Save** the information entered by clicking the `Save` button.

<aside>
💡 In the overview you can also add further fields to your contact such as place of jurisdiction, company register number, management, etc.

</aside>
